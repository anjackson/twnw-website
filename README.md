# twnw-website

Source for the https://twnw.info website

## Why Am I Doing This?

Because I've seen living standards and public sector investment fall and fall over my lifetime, and I'm seen how the distribution of wealth has distorted our democracies. Making it almost impossible to make progress on any of the big issues that face us, and dividing us against each other

## How Do I Hope This Might Be Used?

I hope people will use this to find out more about the nature and consequences of wealth inequality, and what we might do to make things better. How we might be able to come together around this issue, even is we disagree on other matters.

## Why Is This Important?

I think wealth inequality is the biggest issue facing us today, distorting how everything else is handled. The closest thing to a root cause.
